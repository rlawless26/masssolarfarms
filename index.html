What to Expect

Here's what will happen after signing up

  • Quick phone call

    One of our experts will call you to learn about your energy usage and to make sure you qualify for the program.

  • Apply for Community Solar

    One of our specialist will come to your house for a quick 30 minute qualification review. If you qualify, we will enter you into the Mass Save Program!

  • Reserve your panels

    Now it’s time for the fun! An energy expert will conduct a complete home energy assessment. Usually these take 2-3 hours. They will give you a report at the end full of any recommendations to improve your efficiency! And, they’ll give you lots of freebies!

  • Start saving

    Review the expert recommendations and decide which ones to move forward with! With all the rebates and incentives we think you’ll see why almost all homeowners move forward with some sort of improvement.

  • Pat yourself on the back

    Qualified professionals will install your weatherization improvements. Rest easy in your comfortable, healthy and efficient home!
